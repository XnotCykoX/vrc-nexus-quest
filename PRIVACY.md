# VRC-NEXUS (Quest) — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-15_

VRC-NEXUS is a standalone companion app for VRChat. It has **no backend server of our own** — it runs entirely on your device and talks directly to the services below.

## What we collect
**Nothing is sent to us.** We operate no servers, accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking. We do not receive, store, sell, or share any of your data.

## What is stored on your device only
- Your **VRChat login session cookie** (so you stay signed in). Your **password is never stored** — it is sent only to VRChat to obtain the session, exactly like signing in on VRChat's own site.
- Your **favourites, sections, recent avatars, settings, and saved scripts**.
- **Cached avatar metadata** (names/images) for display.

All of the above lives in the app's local storage on your headset and is removed if you uninstall the app or sign out.

## Services the app contacts directly (from your device)
- **VRChat API** (`api.vrchat.cloud`) — to log in and fetch your avatars, friends, worlds, groups and notifications. Governed by VRChat's own privacy policy and Terms.
- **Community avatar databases** (e.g. `avtr.icu`) — only when you use **Discover**, to search public avatars.
- **GitHub** (`raw.githubusercontent.com`) — to check for app updates.
- **VRChat (on your device, via OSC/UDP on `127.0.0.1`)** — for the chatbox, avatar parameters and inputs. OSC traffic never leaves your headset.

## Donations (optional in-app purchase)
The app offers an optional donation add-on through the Meta Horizon Store. This purchase:
- Provides **no in-app goods, currency, unlocks, or functional benefit** — it exists purely as a way for users to voluntarily support development.
- Is processed entirely by **Meta's own In-App Purchase (IAP) system**. We do not build, operate, or have access to any payment processing of our own.
- Involves **no personal or financial data passing through the app or to us**. Meta handles billing, payment details, and transaction records directly; we do not receive your payment method, billing address, or any other checkout information.
- Is **not linked to your VRChat account or any data described elsewhere in this policy** — it is a fully separate transaction handled by Meta's platform.

## Permissions and why
- **Internet** — VRChat API, avatar search, update checks, and local OSC.
- **Read images** — only to show your VRChat screenshots in the Gallery (read-only; images stay on your device).
- **Install packages** — only so the in-app updater can install a newer version you choose to download.

## Data controller / responsible entity
For the purposes of Meta Horizon's data handling declarations, the entity responsible for any Meta Horizon User Data and/or Device User Data made accessible through this app is:

**VRC NEXUS**, located in **Australia**.

We do not use any third-party data processors or service providers with access to Meta Horizon User Data or Device User Data — as noted above, no data is collected, transmitted, or shared with us or anyone else. All processing occurs locally on your device, and the only third parties contacted are VRChat, the avatar databases you choose to browse, and GitHub for update checks, each under their own respective privacy policies.

## Children
VRChat requires users to be 13+. This app is intended for existing VRChat users and is not directed at children under 13.
